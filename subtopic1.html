Sedan

A sedan is a type of car with four doors and a typical seating capacity of four to five persons. It is known for its comfortable and spacious interior, making it a popular choice for families.

Types of Sedan

-Subcompact sedan: small cars with four doors and a trunk, e.g. Nissan Versa, Kia Rio

-Compact sedan: slightly larger than subcompact, e.g. Toyota Corolla, Honda Civic

-Mid-size sedan: medium-sized cars with more space and features, e.g. Nissan Altima, Honda Accord, Toyota Camry

-Full-size sedan: large cars with ample room and power, e.g. Dodge Charger, Toyota Avalon

-Luxury sedan: high-end cars with premium materials and technology, e.g. Mercedes-Benz, Lexus
