Welcome to Car Types

Explore different types of cars and find the one that suits your needs.

Sedan

A sedan is a type of car with four doors and a closed roof. It typically has a separate trunk for luggage and provides comfortable seating for four or more passengers.

SUV

An SUV, or Sport Utility Vehicle, is a versatile vehicle with a spacious interior and often features four-wheel drive. It is designed to handle various terrains and offers ample cargo space.

Coupe

A coupe is a two-door car with a fixed roof and a more compact size. Coupes are known for their sporty appearance and are often associated with high-performance driving.

Convertible

A convertible is a car with a retractable roof that can be folded down, providing an open-air driving experience. Convertibles are popular for their stylish and adventurous appeal.
