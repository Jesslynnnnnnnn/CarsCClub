SUVs

TSUV stands for Sports Utility Vehicle. These vehicles are designed to provide ample cargo space, higher ground clearance, and off-road capabilities. SUVs are versatile and suitable for various lifestyles..

Types of SUVs

-Crossover SUVs: SUVs that are based on car platforms and have better fuel efficiency and handling than truck-based SUVs.

-Mini SUVs: The smallest and least expensive SUVs, usually with limited off-road capabilities.

-Compact SUVs: SUVs that are slightly larger than mini SUVs and offer more cargo space and performance.

-Mid-size SUVs: SUVs that are larger than compact SUVs and can accommodate up to seven passengers and tow moderate loads.

-Full-size SUVs: SUVs that are based on truck platforms and have the most cargo space, passenger capacity, and towing power.
